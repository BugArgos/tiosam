Cognates

In this module, students explore the fascinating
concept of cognates, which are words in different
languages that share a common linguistic origin.
The primary focus is on recognizing and understanding
cognates as a powerful tool for language learners.
The module covers various aspects, including the
benefits of cognate recognition, strategies for
identifying them,and how cognates contribute to language proficiency.

Noun Phrases

This module focuses on the structure and functions
of noun phrases, essential components of language
that play a crucial role in conveying information.
Students explore the concept of noun phrases, which
are groups of words centered around a noun, and delve
into the various elements that can comprise these structures.

Verb Phrase

This module delves into the intricacies of verb
phrases, which are fundamental components of
language that convey actions, states, or occurrences.
Students explore the structure and functions of verb
phrases, gaining insights into the diverse elements
that constitute these essential linguistic units.

Sentence Structure

This module is dedicated to the exploration of
sentence structure, a fundamental aspect of
language that governs the organization of
words to convey meaningful thoughts. Students
delve into the key components of sentences,
gaining a comprehensive understanding of how
words come together to form grammatically
correct and coherent expressions.

Verb Tenses

This module is dedicated to the exploration of
verb tenses, a critical aspect of language that
allows speakers and writers to situate actions or
states in time. Students delve into the various
tenses in English and their functions, gaining a
comprehensive understanding of how verb tenses
contribute to effective communication.
